Target Audience

The target audience is mainly people who want a quick glance at what the weather is going to be like
in their specific areas.

Persona 1:
Susan is a mother of 6 small children, she is a stay at home mom; however, she is
constantly needting to figure out what the weather is to appropriately dress her children for school.
She is also looking for quick advice during different storm warning advisories. She is 50 years old
and her children range from ages 10-3. She is protective over her children and checks the weather at
least daily via her phone to ensure that her and her kids will not be surprised when they are outside.

Persona 2:
Chip is a 21 man who is always going outside to hunt, hike, fish and perform many other
outside activities, because of this he is always looking at the weather forecast in order to best
plan out his next adventure. Generally speaking he is looking at the weather on his phone. Occasionally,
he does end up using his school laptop to look at the weekly forecast.

Scenarios:
1) What will today's weather look like?
2) What is the weekly forecast for my area?
3) What do I do when faced with a natural disaster or storm warning?
4) When will it start raining today?
w 5) What do hurricanes look like?

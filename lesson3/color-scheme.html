Color Scheme

The background color of the website will be #c4f0ff which is a very light shade of blue.
From there we will have a header and footer color of #d51920 which is a deep red.
If you hover over a link the "box" surrounding it will be lightened to a #e88185 which is light pink.
Then general text will be black including but not limited to p tags, h tags, and a tags.

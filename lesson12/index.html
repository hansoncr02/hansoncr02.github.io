Temple Worship

Participating in temple ordinances is a wonderful and important part of your commitment to follow Jesus Christ. It is also an essential step toward someday returning to live with Him and our Heavenly Father. As you prepare for these sacred experiences, there is much you can do to make sure you are ready. Your preparation should include prayerfully studying the gospel of Jesus Christ and applying those teachings in your life. The foundation of your preparation for the temple will be to faithfully honor the sacred covenants you have already made.

Our Hotels

Temple Inn has been providing exemplary housing for the many members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints for over 100 years now. Located within two miles of select temples we allow our guests to feel the spirit both inside and out of the temple. Each of our residents is asked and required to comply with church standards and etiquette. Creating a zion type environment for all who enter.

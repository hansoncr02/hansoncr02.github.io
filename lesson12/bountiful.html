Bountiful Temple

Plans to construct the temple were announced May 28, 1988, Church leaders broke ground May 2, 1992, and Howard W. Hunter, president of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints from 1994 to 1995, dedicated the Bountiful Utah Temple January 8-14, 1995. The temple serves Latter-day Saints in central and south Davis County.

The exterior of the 104,000-square-foot temple is Bethel white Granite.

Currently, there are over 50 temples in various stages of construction, renovation or have been announced throughout the world.
